„Who can afford to waive a market share of 25%?”

06/18/2019

AUTOHAUS journal interviews cotedo experts on electric mobility

The manufacturers push them, the customers want them and the politicians support them – and yet, electric vehicles are still rather unpopular in garages and the car trade. This is going to change: no dealership can afford to waive a market share of 25%! It is high time the dealers regarded electric mobility as a chance, and got the right charging infrastructure, workshop equipment and staff qualification under way.

In order to provide competent information for its readers, the German journal AUTOHAUS published a detailed interview with our experts for the automotive after-sales and for charging infrastructure, Dennis Hartmann and Andreas Lemke.
