Charging infrastructure – implementation of OEM specifications

05/21/2019

cotedo provides professional planning for car dealerships

“What charging infrastructure should I buy for my car dealership?” That is a very common question these days. The brand-associated dealers have to obey the vehicle manufacturers’ specifications. However, some specs seem not quite thought through, and consequently raise a lot of questions. Multi-brand companies struggle even more: they have to reconcile various sets of (partially inscrutable) instructions. And then there is the question regarding the power supply and the right choice of product…

Our customers can lean back and relax – they benefit from our experience and our market insight. cotedo delivers an individual planning package for each car dealership, entirely independent of manufacturers and brands. We incorporate the current situation, OEM specifications, supply data and any existing charging points. A concrete, step-by-step guideline leads to a successful implementation. Thanks to our valid assessment, the costs are absolutely transparent right from the start.
