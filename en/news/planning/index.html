Planning phase almost completed, let’s get building!

09/19/2019

BMW builds new branch with the help of cotedo

For the past months, we have been supporting BMW with a multitude of designs, drafts and all our knowledge about car dealerships. Their new branch in Nuremberg is a mammoth project with 12,500 square meters! The planning phase is now almost completed: at the beginning of October 2019, the board of directors will formally approve the concept.

During the realization phase, BMW can count on cotedo, too. Aftersales specialist André Glanz explains: “Service, warehouse and workshop must work hand in hand. We are experts in car dealerships. This is why our building and equipment specialists can make sure that both the productive area and the warehouse of the new branch will be put into reality in an optimal way.”
