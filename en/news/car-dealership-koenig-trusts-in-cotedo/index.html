Berlin-based car dealership König & Platen entrusts cotedo with its new building project

From first concept to grand opening: Berlin-based car dealership König & Platen entrusts cotedo with its new building project.

The long-established enterprise König & Platen has commissioned cotedo to oversee the planning and realisation of its new building project in Berlin. The Volvo and Hyundai dealership is going to meet all the up-to-date standards and will be one of Germany’s first partners in the new „Volvo Personal Service“ concept. The opening ceremony is planned for this year.

“König & Platen trusts our know-how and has taken cotedo on board as early as during the concept phase. Therefore we can make sure that the new facility is structured and equipped in the best possible way – right from the start “, explains Dennis Hartmann, team leader Aftersales Consulting.

Years of cooperation with various brands qualify cotedo as an ideal partner regarding manufacturer standards and optimal building dimensions. With its new functional workshop equipment, the dealership is now ready for current challenges like digitalisation and e-mobility. It also complies with all the requirements for the new Volvo service concept.
