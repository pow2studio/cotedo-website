“In German, please“: cotedo accounts for German marketing material for Pro-Cut International

05/19/2016

Pro-Cut International is a renowned manufacturer of workshop equipment from the USA. For the distribution of their brake disc optimisation system PFM 9.2 BOSS in Germany/Austria/Switzerland, cotedo developed the German marketing material.

cotedo was responsible for the transfer of the manuals and the technical data into German. Sales sheets and a German website complete the marketing mix.

We say thank you for another wonderful opportunity to show our marketing competence in workshop equipment, this time in an international context.
