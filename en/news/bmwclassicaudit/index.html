“Kompetencja” for vintage vehicles

08/13/2018

cotedo audits first BMW Classic workshop in Poland

Since July 2018, cotedo has been active in Poland, too: the long-established dealership Fus in Warsaw had asked to be certified as a BMW Classic Partner. They present BMW, MINI, Rolls-Royce, BMW Motorrad and BMW Alpina, and now would like to set-up a competence centre for classic vehicles.

“Evaluating special workshops for vintage vehicles requires profound knowledge, practical experience in workshops and a distinct hands-on mentality”, explains Thorsten Binder, who is responsible for the Classic program at cotedo. “The audit was very successful. I expect the dealership will receive their certification this summer”, says Binder.

cotedo compiled the BMW Classic Standards back in 2016. Since then, we have been auditing more than 40 dealerships in Germany and in Europe. We are glad that Fus in Warsaw now also benefits from our know-how. Very soon, they will be able to publicly showcase their competence on their dealership sign.
