New colleagues for our Order Management team

07/11/2016

We are happy to welcome two highly skilled and dedicated colleagues as new members of our Order Management team.

During the past few weeks, Raimund Kupecek and Christina Mautz have successfully learned the ropes of their new tasks. Both of them can look back on years of experience in both international order management and parts logistics in the automotive industry.

By thus extending our capacities, we can offer our customers even more expert support for the order management of their products. We bid our new colleagues a warm welcome!
