The place to be for “appassionati di auto classiche“

12/11/2019

cotedo audits BMW Classic Partner in Brescia, Italy

With the Italian car dealership Nanni Nember, cotedo audited a “natural” BMW Classic Partner in autumn 2019. The Nember family have been repairing BMW vehicles for three generations and to cap it all off, they are situated in Brescia, the start/finish point of the legendary Mille Miglia race for vintage cars.

For many years, the establishment has been serving as a repair base for the BMW teams taking part in the race, and it generally enjoys a good reputation among local historic car enthusiasts. “The BMW Classic certification was a logical consequence,” says Thorsten Binder, cotedo’s classic car expert. Congratulations to the brand new BMW Classic Partner Nanni Nember, and “molto successo continua”!
