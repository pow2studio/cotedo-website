Aftersales competence for your building project

11/14/2017

cotedo in special issue of magazine AUTOHAUS

The German magazine AUTOHAUS publishes an annual special issue focussing on the planning, building and equipment of car dealerships. Anyone planning a new building or refurbishment in this sector will find their best project partners there. We are happy to announce that we were able to place a portrait of cotedo on page 62 of this year’s issue.

On one whole page, we are presenting our services in the field of Automotive Aftersales Consulting. Our support relieves architects and planning teams since we take over both the commercial and the technical planning process. We are 100% independent from manufacturers and workshop fitters. Therefore we can take over the responsibility for the entire project. We know: our custom-made solutions provide maximum protection for our customers’ investment.
